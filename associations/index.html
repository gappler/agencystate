This is AI you own.

Your CRM may already have AI. But you can’t take it outside the platform or apply it to problems the vendor didn’t anticipate.

Claude and Claude Code are different. A Claude project configured with your brand guidelines, your audience data, and your workflow logic is not a feature inside someone else’s software. It’s your system. You can see every prompt, change it, and push the output back into your stack.

Four sessions. Four builds.

Session 01

Your knowledge base

Build a Claude knowledge base using simulated data — a generic association or one modeled on yours. Learn the method before applying it to live work.

Session 02

The work that repeats

Pick your most time-eating recurring work. Build a Claude-powered workflow for it. Renewal. Conference. Chapter. Committee.

Session 03

Content in your voice

Teach Claude your association’s voice, audiences, and rules. Build on-brand member emails, newsletters, speaker outreach — not generic AI.

Session 04

Demo day

Present the workflows and outputs you built for your actual work. Peer feedback. You leave capable of doing this alone.

What you take with you

Your association’s knowledge base. Claude-powered workflows for your recurring work — yours to run, extend, and share internally. The method to keep building as your needs change. No subscription. No vendor lock.
