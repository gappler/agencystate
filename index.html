What we do

Cohort-based AI intensives.

Small groups learning together. You build tools for a simulated company you define, learning the method in a controlled environment before applying it to your own work. You'll leave with working examples, practical outputs, and the skills to keep building.

How we do it

Context before tools.

We start with business context and build from there.

01

Define your business context

Build a complete simulated company — brand, audience, data, objectives. This is the foundation that makes everything downstream specific and useful.

02

Build tools that inform decisions

Strategic plans, scenario models, dashboards — each one feeds the next. You learn to write prompts independently and build sequentially.

03

Produce content and practical outputs

Content engines, reports, briefs, and files your existing systems can use. This is where the method starts producing deliverables you can use immediately.

04

Workshop your real problems

Bring a real problem. The group pressure-tests it live — scope, context, traps. You leave with a plan of your own and fresh thinking on nine more.
