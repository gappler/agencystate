Learn Claude Code. Build real tools. Own the outcome.

Agency State teaches professionals how to partner with Claude Code and related tools — how to capture what your team knows and needs, and put it to work.

For you, this could mean campaign strategy, content production, competitive intelligence, project planning, client reporting, or a website like this one — built from your brand, your data, and your instruction.

What we do

Cohort-based intensives.

Small groups learning the methodology together. Participants build tools that capture their team's context and produce outputs their existing systems can use — project plans, content matrices, structured briefs, importable files. Everyone leaves with working examples and the skills to keep building.

Participants arrive with prework completed and start building immediately. The intensive is structured around doing, not watching.

Who is this for? For professionals who have to get things done, are not afraid of tech, and know that mastering AI tools is essential for ongoing professional success.

Team implementation support and ongoing advisory are available for organizations that want the methodology applied directly to their operations.

How we do it

Foundations first.

We start with a business context and build out from there.

01

Define your business context

Build a complete business simulation — brand, audience, data, objectives. This is the foundation that makes everything downstream specific and useful.

02

Build tools that inform decisions

Planning tools, scenario models, budget allocators — each one feeds the next. You learn to write prompts independently and build sequentially.

03

Produce content and practical outputs

Content engines, reports, briefs, and files your existing systems can use. Plus a first look at connecting to live data.

04

Build for your team

Bring your own data. Build a tool that solves a real problem on your team. Present it to the group. Discuss.
