Agency State teaches business and marketing professionals to build working tools with Claude Code — dashboards, planners, content engines, reports, even websites like this one — using your own brand, data, and strategy. No engineering background required.

That’s agency — the ability to act on your own terms. Build what you need, when you need it, without waiting on someone else.

What we do

Cohort-based AI intensives.

Small groups learning the methodology together. Participants build tools for a simulated company they define — dashboards, planners, content engines, reports — learning the methodology in a controlled environment before applying it to their own work. Everyone leaves with working examples, practical outputs, and the skills to keep building.

Who is this for? Business and marketing professionals who are hands-on with technology and need to do more with less.

How we do it

Foundations first.

We start with business context and build from there.

01

Define your business context

Build a complete simulated company — brand, audience, data, objectives. This is the foundation that makes everything downstream specific and useful.

02

Build tools that inform decisions

Planning tools, scenario models, budget allocators — each one feeds the next. You learn to write prompts independently and build sequentially.

03

Produce content and practical outputs

Content engines, reports, briefs, and files your existing systems can use. This is where the methodology starts producing deliverables you can use immediately.

04

Build for your team

Bring your own data. Build a tool that solves a real problem on your team. Present it to the group. Discuss.
